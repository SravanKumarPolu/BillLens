## Permissions

BillLens may request the following permissions:

- **Purpose**: Take photos of bills for OCR processing
- **Usage**: Only when you tap the camera button
- **Storage**: Images stored locally, not uploaded anywhere

### Photo Library (Optional)
- **Purpose**: Select bill images from your gallery
- **Usage**: Only when you choose to select an image
- **Storage**: Images stored locally

### SMS (Optional - Android only)
- **Purpose**: Auto-read payment confirmation SMS for bill detection
- **Usage**: Only when you explicitly enable this feature
- **Privacy**: SMS data processed locally, never shared
- **Control**: You can disable anytime in settings

All permissions are **optional** â€” you can use BillLens without granting any permissions (you'll just need to enter expenses manually).
